PETER BENJAMIN PARKER
Famously known as Spider-Man from Earth-120703, is a beloved Marvel Comics character imbued with superhuman abilities after being bitten by a radioactive spider. He juggles his dual identity, fighting crime while navigating the complexities of his personal life in New York City.

Andrew Garfield took on the role of Peter Parker/Spider-Man in "The Amazing Spider-Man" film series, embodying the character's emotional depth and internal conflicts. While his portrayal received mixed reviews, Garfield's dedication to depicting Peter's struggles with identity and relationships resonated with some audiences, adding a unique perspective to the Spider-Man legacy in cinema.
PETER PARKER
In the Marvel Cinematic Universe (Earth-199999), Peter Parker, better known as Spider-Man, graces the screen with a refreshing blend of youthful exuberance and unwavering determination. Portrayed by Tom Holland, Parker's journey is characterized by the struggles of balancing his double life as a high school student and a superhero.

As Spider-Man, His youthful energy and endearing charisma breathe new life into the character, making him both relatable and aspirational. Holland's Spider-Man is defined not only by his incredible superhuman abilities but also by his vulnerability and humanity, as he grapples with the weight of his responsibilities and the complexities of his personal life.

In the Earth-96283 universe, Peter Parker, portrayed by Tobey Maguire, becomes Spider-Man after being bitten by a genetically modified spider. The trilogy consists of "Spider-Man" (2002), "Spider-Man 2" (2004), and "Spider-Man 3" (2007).

Tobey Maguire's portrayal of Spider-Man is often praised for its earnestness and emotional depth. He captures the essence of Peter Parker's journey from an awkward teenager to a responsible superhero facing personal and moral dilemmas. Overall, the Earth-96283 Spider-Man trilogy is considered a classic in the superhero genre, with Tobey Maguire's portrayal leaving a lasting impact on fans and influencing subsequent adaptations of the character.
MILES MORALES
In Earth-1610, also known as the Ultimate Marvel universe, Miles Morales takes on the mantle of Spider-Man. Miles Morales, unlike Peter Parker, is a half-black, half-Latino teenager from Brooklyn who gains spider-like abilities after being bitten by a genetically enhanced spider. His journey as Spider-Man is characterized by a strong sense of responsibility and a desire to honor the legacy of the original Spider-Man, Peter Parker.

Miles Morales has gained prominence beyond the comic books, appearing in animated television shows, video games, and the Academy Award-winning animated film "Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse" (2018). His portrayal has been praised for its authenticity, relatability, and cultural significance, inspiring audiences of all backgrounds and ages.
